Michael Sokolar

Author · CEO · Vienna

I live just outside Vienna with my wife and two daughters. By day I run Little Lights Studio, a brand film agency with eleven years of experience making films for companies with something real to say. By night I write middle-grade fantasy.

The Helena Flinn Chronicles began as a short story about goblin dream engineers manufacturing dreams in a lab. The goblins came first. Then the city beneath Vienna. Then Helena. The world grew around them over years of writing.
